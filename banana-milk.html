Banana Milkshake Recipe

Indulge in the simplicity and deliciousness of a classic banana milkshake. This delightful beverage combines creamy texture with the natural sweetness of ripe bananas, making it a perfect treat for any time of the day. Whether youâ€™re enjoying it as a quick breakfast, a refreshing afternoon pick-me-up, or a dessert, this banana milkshake is sure to satisfy your cravings. Its refreshing nature, especially when chilled, makes it an ideal companion on warm days or whenever you need a little comfort in a glass.

Ingredients

  • 150 ml milk
  • 1 banana
  • 2-4 ice cubes crushed

Instructions

  • Start by peeling and slicing the banana into small chunks.
  • Place the banana pieces into your blender. Next, pour 150ml of milk into the blender with the banana
  • Add the crushed ice cubes as well.
  • Blend all the ingredients together until the mixture is smooth and the ice cubes have fully dissolved.
  • Pour into a glass and enjoy your refreshing banana milkshake.
  • garnish it with some banana slices or your favourite dry fruits
